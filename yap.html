My love for Cars

One huge thing about me is that I like cars. Like old Volvos, trucks, sports cars. I love the rumble that big engines make. I like more of the mean sounding ones which is typically from American V8’s. Italian V12 are ok but doesn’t sound quite as mean. It’s also something about the fact that a man-made object can be capable of such great speeds. And reach those speeds in a matter of seconds. Driving also is relaxing I find. Sadly, nowadays being an car enthusiast is harder and harder with gas prices, car prices, and more electric cars being implemented.
